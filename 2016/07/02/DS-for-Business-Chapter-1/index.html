Data Science for Business Chapter 1 reading notes

Example: Hurricane Frances

Wal-Mart Stores 通过以往飓风到来时消费者购物记录，预测飓风即将到来时将物品销售量(New York Times, 2004)。

P: why data-driven prediction might be useful in this scenario?
预测一些显性的因素 could be somewhat useful. 真正需要的是 discover patterns to identify unusual local demand for products under similar situations (such as 以往飓风到来时).

Example: Predicting Customer Churn

definition: Customers switching from one company to another is called churn.
在特定区域 communication firms 希望顾客 retention in business 当合约到期，而不是转投其他公司。吸引新客户的花费多于保留老客户。

task: 给定 retention offer, 挖掘适用于 retention offer 的客户。

P: what data you might use and how they would be used?
后面几章慢慢研究。。。

Data Science, Engineering, and Data-Driven Decision Making

two types of DDD problem:
1: decisions for which “discoveries” need to be made within data, 既确定目标 2: increases accuracy based on data analysis.

关于1，挖掘先于其他人的目标，如一个家庭的购物清单会因为新生儿而变化，故可以挖掘那些有新生儿诞生的家庭，但更先一步，挖掘已经怀孕的家庭，甚至准备怀孕的家庭，进行物品的推广。
关于2， improve ability to estimate

Data Processing and “Big Data”

Data Processing and “Big Data” 不等于 Data science, 关系如figure 1-1所示. 但使用Big Data 可有效提高生产率(economist Prasanna Tambe of NYU’s Stern School). 精确地说 one standard deviation higher utilization of big data technologies is associated with 1%–3% higher productivity than the average firm; one standard deviation lower in terms of big data utilization is associated with 1%–3% lower productivity.

Data and Data Science Capability as a Strategic Asset

one of the fundamental principles of data science: data, and the capability to extract useful knowledge from data, should be regarded as key strategic
assets.

Data-Analytic Thinking

the principles of data-analytic thinking 很重要，具体的后面一章讲。

This Book

此书算法少，主要讲基本概念和思想。

Data Mining and Data Science, Revisited

Fundamental concept:
1: Extracting useful knowledge from data to solve business problems can be treated systematically by following a process with reasonably well-defined stages. 2: From a large mass of data, information technology can be used to
find informative descriptive attributes of entities of interest.
3: If you look too hard at a set of data, you will find something—but
it might not generalize beyond the data you’re looking at. 4: Formulating data mining solutions and evaluating the results involves thinking carefully about the context in which they will be used.

Chemistry Is Not About Test Tubes: Data Science Versus the Work of the Data Scientist

主要讲 data science 在出书时的状况，是一个全新的领域，以及该书的侧重点为 science not on the technology.
